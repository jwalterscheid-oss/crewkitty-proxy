What CrewKitty is

CrewKitty is a software service that helps small businesses control employee spending. A company loads a set dollar amount onto each employee's account and chooses which businesses that money may be spent at. Each employee gets a virtual company card (usable in Apple Pay or Google Pay) that only works at approved merchants and only up to their available balance. Owners can add funds, freeze a card, or remove an employee at any time.

CrewKitty is operated by JA Oilfield Services LLC. It is a spend-management and expense-control tool — we do not sell physical goods.

What you get

Management-set limits

Employees can spend only the amount you load — overspending is declined automatically.

Approved businesses only

Cards work only at the merchants on your list. Add or remove them anytime.

Instant control

Freeze a card or remove a departing employee in one click; remaining funds return to the company.

No physical cards

Virtual cards live in Apple Pay and Google Pay — nothing to print, mail, or activate.

Pricing

Free to sign up · 1% per transaction

Creating a company account and adding employees and businesses is free. CrewKitty charges a 1% software fee on each approved employee card transaction. No monthly fees, no setup fees.

How it works

1. Create your free company account.
2. Add the businesses your crew is allowed to use.
3. Add employees with a PIN and a starting amount — a virtual card is issued instantly.
4. Employees sign in on their phones, see their balance, and add the card to Apple Pay or Google Pay.
5. Top up, freeze, or remove cards anytime from your console.
