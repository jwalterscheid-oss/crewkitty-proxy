Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026. CrewKitty is operated by JA Oilfield Services LLC.

What we collect

Company account details (name, email), employee names and the spending limits you set, the businesses you approve, and transaction records (amount, merchant, date). We do not collect or store full card numbers — card data is held by our payment provider, Stripe.

How we use it

To operate the service: authorizing or declining card transactions against your rules, showing balances and history, and calculating fees. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

We share data with Stripe, Inc. to issue cards and process payments, and with infrastructure providers (Supabase, Vercel) that host the application. These providers are bound to protect the data.

Retention & your rights

We keep records while your account is active and as required for tax and compliance. To access or delete your data, email us.
